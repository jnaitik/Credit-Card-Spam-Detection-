📘 Guide to Using the Fraud Detection System

🔍 How Credit Card Fraud Happens

Fraudulent actors may steal card information through phishing, data breaches, or skimming devices. They perform unauthorized transactions online or in-store.

💡 Features Used in Detection

  • creditLimit – The total credit limit on the card
  • availableCredit – Current unused credit
  • amount – Transaction amount
  • paymentDueInDays – Days left before bill is due
  • lastPaymentAmount – Amount paid in last billing cycle
  • transactionType – e.g., "purchase", "cash_advance", "payment"
  • merchantCategoryCode – Type of merchant (e.g., grocery, travel)
  • geoLocation – Region of transaction
  • timeOfDay – Morning, afternoon, evening, or night
  • isCardPresent – True if card was physically present
  • isForeignTransaction – True if transaction is international
  • isFlaggedFraud – Manually flagged as suspicious by bank

🧠 How AI Detects Fraud

The system analyzes behavior patterns and flags high-risk transactions. For example:

  • ⚠️ Large amounts with card not present
  • 🌍 International transaction with low balance
  • 🚩 Manually flagged as fraud

🛡️ How This App Helps

It gives an instant prediction using AI with a human-style explanation to help you understand why a transaction is safe or risky.
